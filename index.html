ABOUT US


"AGTECH HACKATHON" is aimed at applying innovative solutions to problems in the agricultural sector, integrating technologies and innovations in agriculture. In addition to learning how these key concepts are used in agrarian areas, participants will also gain the best experience and skills they do not use to add value to an organization.

Hackathon


Hackathon will continue for 3 days in the direction of the development of agaric industry, development of prototypes, application of innovative technologies. Students, researchers, designers, entrepreneurs, companies, job seekers can participate in hackathon. Participants will work as a team and they must apply innovative solutions to various problems in the field of Agriculture. The team that presents the best solution in the agrarian field will be the winner.

Challenges


1. Challenge

How can we leverage technology to train/upskill farm workers and potentially attract new workers to farming?

2. Challenge

How to identify field crops based on satellite imagery data that is useful for infrastructure optimization?

3. Challenge

How to create the efficient agricultural statistics with open data digitalization, verification and visualization?
